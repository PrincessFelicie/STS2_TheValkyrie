The Valkyrie is a character mod for Slay The Spire 2.

 - Armor up to handle multi-hit attacks
 - Make your enemies Bleed out
 - Bless your cards with Enchantments to get the most out of them
 - Smite your way up to the top of the Spire
 - But beware Overexertion -- putting yourself into harm's way risks making you more fragile than ever!
